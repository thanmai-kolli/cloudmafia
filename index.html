Welcome to Cloud Mafia

Cloud Mafia is the exciting social deduction game, where deception and strategy meet. Play with your friends, be assigned a secret role, and switch through the shadows trying to be a mafia, detective, medic, or an innocent civilian. Never put your trust in people.

Game Rules

  • Minimum 7 players to start a room.
  • Each player is assigned a secret role.
  • The game alternates between Night and Day phases.
  • At Night, the mafia selects a target, and the medic chooses someone to save.
  • During the Day, players discuss and vote to eliminate one suspected player.
  • The game continues until the mafia is eliminated or they outnumber civilians.
